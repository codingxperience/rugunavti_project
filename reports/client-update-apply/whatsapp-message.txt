Client update - Ruguna College application page

Implemented and polished the admissions form into a more complete applicant flow:

1. Brand updated to Ruguna College with the motto One Who Prevails.
2. Application entry routes now cover Certificate, Diploma, Bachelor's, and Short Course Interest.
3. Required fields are clearly marked with asterisks.
4. Personal details now include gender, date of birth, nationality, and disability question.
5. WhatsApp, alternative phone, and next-of-kin phone now support country codes for international applicants.
6. Next-of-kin details were added for student support and admissions follow-up.
7. Education background now captures previous programme and class/result level.
8. Credit transfer, referral source, document upload choice, and confirmation check were added.
9. The side guidance cards were cleaned so the page reads better and does not feel derailed.
